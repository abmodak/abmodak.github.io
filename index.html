About Me

I am a researcher in high energy nuclear physics working on the properties of strongly interacting matter produced in high-energy collisions. My research focuses on small systems, collectivity, and jet-related observables within the ALICE experiment at CERN.

Research Highlights

Collectivity in Small Systems

Study of long-range correlations and multiplicity-dependent particle production in pp and p–Pb collisions.

Jet and Correlation Studies

Exploring connections between hard probes and soft particle production to understand QCD dynamics.
