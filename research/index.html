Research Highlights

Jet substructure in small systems

Investigation of medium-induced modifications of jet structure in high-multiplicity pp and p–Pb collisions.

Key result: First constraints on QGP-like effects in small systems.

Collectivity and flow observables

Study of long-range correlations and anisotropic flow coefficients to probe collective behavior.

Key result: Evidence of collective expansion in pp collisions.
